How to play

Kingdom for the Word! is a word-game inspired by the story of King Richard the Third as told in William Shakespeare's drama. There a horse was worth the kindgdom when it was badly needed. Maybe you know the feeling when you're in search of a missing word?!

Kindgom for the Word! is fantastic for exercising linguistic skills!

Type a word related to the one shown on screen.

If your guess is in the thesaurus you score a point.

You have a limited number of tries in one round depending on your game choice.

Enjoy the game!
